Indoor Branding

Wherever you go there is opportunity for effective exposure – whether it’s a large or a small space, we have a range of indoor solutions to give your brand a powerful presence.

We offer a wide range of systems to suit every occasion. We both manufacture locally and import directly. These systems can be used wherever ideas are to be presented individually, functionally and professionally, such as: Exhibitions, Sports Meetings and in the foyers of Companies and Public buildings. Our assortment of products is rounded off with a variety of specially designed accessories such as Lighting Systems and Transport Bags. We can reprint panels for existing hardware at competitive prices.

Our products are engineered to be lightweight, compact and portable, and are available in a variety of sizes.
